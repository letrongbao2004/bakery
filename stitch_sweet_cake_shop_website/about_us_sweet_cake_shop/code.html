Our Story

Crafting moments of joy through artisanal baking since 2019.

Câu chuyện thương hiệu

Sweet Cake Shop began with a simple belief: every celebration deserves a masterpiece. Born from a small home kitchen, our passion for combining traditional French techniques with local flavors quickly grew into something extraordinary.

We source only the finest ingredients—from rich Belgian chocolate to fresh, seasonal fruits—ensuring that every bite is not just a taste, but an experience.

Our Mission

To elevate everyday moments into memorable celebrations through the artistry of baking, delivering uncompromising quality and sweet joy in every box.

Our Journey

The milestones that shaped Sweet Cake Shop.

2019

The Foundation

Sweet Cake Shop was founded in a humble home kitchen. What started as baking for friends and family quickly blossomed into a local favorite, driven by word-of-mouth and a passion for authentic flavors.

2021

First Flagship Store

We opened our doors to the public with our very first flagship store in the heart of the city. The space was designed to offer an immersive, editorial dessert experience.

2023

Culinary Excellence Award

Recognized by the National Pastry Association for our innovative flavor combinations and dedication to artisanal techniques, cementing our place as a premium dessert destination.

trophy

2025

Digital Degustation

Launching our premium online boutique, bringing the luxurious Sweet Cake Shop experience to homes nationwide with bespoke packaging and temperature-controlled delivery.
